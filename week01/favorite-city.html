My Favorite City

Eskilstuna, Sweden

My favorite city would have to be a moderately sized town found within the mid-interior of Sweden called Eskilstuna. It is not a terribly large town, but it is a charming little area I had the pleasure to serve in on my mission. It has a handful of ancient cathedrals and churches, a river going through the middle of the town and vast fileds of Rapsolja fields that give way to endless evergreen pine forests. The town square is a delight to see, surrounded by various shops, cafes and bakeries. Perfect for a quick afternoon fika.
