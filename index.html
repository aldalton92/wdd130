Utah

Utah is my home. It has a vast and diverse natural beauty ranging from high alpine forests and mountains, to deep red rock desert. It's natural wonders are second to none in my humble opinion. But what shines brighter than all it's natural beauty is the light of it's people, and those that came before. Through hardship, strife, and turmoil it's people have continued to cling unto the light and truth given them of the Lord. Their faithful diligence and the sacrifices they made are a sacred heritage unto all who come unto the Lord, and join themselves unto his one true living church. This is why I love to call this place my home.
