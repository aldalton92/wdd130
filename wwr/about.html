Raven Whiteriver Rafting

At Raven Whiteriver Rafting, we bring people closer to the wild spirit of the river through safe, unforgettable adventures. We value teamwork, courage, and respect for nature, creating experiences that inspire and connect. Ride the current. Respect the wild. Return renewed.

History

Raven Whiteriver Rafting was founded by a small group of river guides who shared a passion for whitewater and a deep respect for the outdoors. What began as a handful of seasonal trips quickly grew through word of mouth and a commitment to safety, skill, and unforgettable experiences.

Over time, we expanded our routes and refined our craft, always guided by the rhythms of the river. Today, we continue that tradition—welcoming adventurers of all levels while staying true to our roots on the water.
